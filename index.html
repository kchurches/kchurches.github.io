Once I found employment, I was able to move out of my mom's house and into my own apartment. Shortly thereafter, I met and began dating my wonderful boyfriend, Kyle, whom I moved in with in February of 2020.

In December of 2020, Kyle and I were lucky enough to move into our very first house! She has a long history (101 years of history, in fact) and we're always discovering new issues but overall, we couldn't be happier.

During this time, I bounced around between a couple different marketing jobs, struggling to find where I fit best. I was discovering that the passion I had for marketing during college was not transferring over into a career as I had hoped. Due to the consistent out-of-place feeling I was experiencing in my day-to-day responsibilities at my job, in February 2021 I decided to quit and take a leap of faith!

I had taken basic lessons in computer programming in high school and had been casually streaming on Twitch as I played video games for a couple months beginning in mid 2020. Suddenly, after I left my job, these two hobbies became the bulk of my day. I now am a Twitch Affiliate and am proficient in the three basic coding languages needed to build practically any website (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript).
