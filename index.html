about me


My boyfriend Kyle & I

Hello, and thank you for visiting my website! My name is Kristyn Churches but I usually go by Kris. I have a Bachelor of Arts in Advertising from Michigan State University and have previous employment experience working as a marketing coordinator, a retail worker, and a kennel worker at a veterinarian's office, among other things.


After I graduated from MSU in December of 2016, I bounced around between a couple different marketing jobs, struggling to find where I fit best. I was discovering that the passion I had for marketing during college was not transferring over into a career as I had hoped. Because of this, I decided to quit the marketing industry in February of 2021 and take a big leap of faith to chase an interest in web development that had started to grow many years before!


I had taken basic classes in website development in high school and had always wanted to work to continue to grow those skills. After much hard work and hours of self-teaching, I am now proficient in the two basic coding languages and have also studied additional programming languages.


My goal now is to continue my improvement as a web developer and designer. While this is not what I had originally intended to do with my life, I am very happy and am hopeful that my growth and success can continue for many years!
