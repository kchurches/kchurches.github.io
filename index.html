about me


Hello, and thank you for visiting my website! My name is Kristyn Churches but usually I go by Kris. I have a Bachelor of Arts in Advertising from Michigan State University and have previous employment experience working as a marketing coordinator, a retail worker, and a kennel worker at a veterinarian's office, among other things.


After I graduated from MSU in December of 2016, I moved home to live with my mom to save some money - and ended up staying for over a year (thanks, mom!). During that time, I worked as an accounts payable bookkeeper while I searched for a marketing job that matched my experience and interests. Once I found employment in the marketing field, I was able to move out of my mom's house and into my own apartment. Shortly thereafter, I met and began dating my wonderful boyfriend, Kyle, whom I moved in with in February of 2020.


In December of 2020, Kyle and I were lucky enough to move into our very first house! She has a long history (101 years of history, in fact) and we're always discovering new issues but overall, we couldn't be happier.


During this time, I bounced around between a couple different marketing jobs, struggling to find where I fit best. I was discovering that the passion I had for marketing during college was not transferring over into a career as I had hoped. Due to a consistent out-of-place feeling I was experiencing in my day-to-day responsibilities at my job, I decided to quit and take a big leap of faith in February of 2021!


I had taken basic lessons in website development in high school and had been casually streaming on Twitch for a couple months beginning in mid 2020. Suddenly, after I left my job, these two hobbies became the bulk of my day. I now am a Twitch Affiliate and am proficient in the three basic coding languages and have also studied additional programming languages.


My goal now is to continue improving as both a web developer and as a Twitch content creator. While neither of these pursuits are what I had originally intended to do with my life, I am very happy and am hopeful that my growth and success can continue for many years!
